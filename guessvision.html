Entertainment · Playable Layers

Turn any feed into a guessing engine.

GuessVision wraps a guessing layer around images, clips, and timelines — turning passive feeds into playable experiences. It’s built for creators, streamers, and studios who want their audience to feel like they’re inside the show, not just watching it.

What GuessVision can wrap

  • Image feeds (archives, galleries, timelines).
  • Short‑form clips and highlight reels.
  • Live streams with audience participation.
  • Historical or genealogical timelines.
Use Cases

GuessVision is a format engine.

A few ways it can show up in the wild — wherever an audience has something to guess, GuessVision has a layer to add.

  • “Guess the year” from a stream of photos or footage.
  • “Guess the outcome” before a clip finishes.
  • “Guess the connection” between people, places, or events.
  • “Guess the next frame” in a sequence or animation.
Why It Belongs in the Entertainment OS

Not just a game — a way of seeing content.
