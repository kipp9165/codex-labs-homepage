DeerSafe — Wildlife Detection Assistant

AI Thermal Detection | Prevent Deer & Wildlife Collisions | Road Safety Technology

DeerSafe is an AI-powered wildlife detection system that protects drivers from deer and large animal collisions using thermal sensing and on-device machine learning. With over 2.1 million deer-vehicle collisions occurring annually in the United States and upward of $10 billion in damages, DeerSafe addresses one of the most persistent and underserved road safety hazards facing rural and suburban drivers today.

Why DeerSafe

Headlights illuminate roughly 50 meters of road at highway speed. A deer can cross that distance in under two seconds. DeerSafe sees 200 meters into the darkness and issues a warning before the threat is visible to the driver. For anyone who regularly drives rural routes, commutes at dawn or dusk, or has already experienced a wildlife collision, DeerSafe is the detection layer that changes the outcome.
