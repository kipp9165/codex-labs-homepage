DeerSafe — Codex Labs

AI-powered wildlife detection for safer roads

DeerSafe provides near-real-time hazard awareness for drivers on rural and suburban roads, using thermal sensing and edge AI classification to identify wildlife risk before impact.

See how it works View Codex Products

How DeerSafe works

Thermal sensing

Detects animal heat signatures beyond normal headlight visibility in low-light conditions.

On-device inference

Classifies wildlife quickly at the edge for low latency alerts without cloud dependency.

Driver alerts

Issues clear warnings when trajectory and distance indicate meaningful collision risk.

Built for practical safety outcomes

DeerSafe is developed as part of the Codex Labs governance-first stack: measurable, understandable, and designed to support safer travel decisions in high-risk environments.
