Visual Detection · Rural Substrate

See deer and anomalies before they hit the road.

DeerSafe is a visual detection instrument tuned for New England backroads, yards, and rural driveways. It’s built to spot deer and other anomalies in your environment before they become collisions, near‑misses, or insurance calls.

What DeerSafe watches for

  • Deer near roads, driveways, and yard edges.
  • Unexpected motion in camera feeds.
  • Patterns of risk over time in the same location.
  • Moments that deserve a second look before you pull out.
How it fits into your world

Built for where the road goes dark.

DeerSafe is designed for people who live where the road is dark, the deer are real, and the nearest streetlight is a town away. It’s not a toy — it’s a quiet, always‑on layer of visual intelligence for the places you actually drive.

  • Runs on a desktop or dedicated field machine.
  • Connects to cameras, feeds, or still images.
  • Flags frames and segments that look risky.
  • Built for clarity, not for noise.
Modes · Operator & Consumer

Two editions. One instrument.

Whether you’re running a municipal road department or just trying to get home safely, DeerSafe has a path for you.

Operator Edition

  • Real‑time monitoring of multiple feeds.
  • Configurable thresholds and alerting.
  • Designed for towns, departments, and operators.

Consumer Edition

  • Photo‑based detection service.
  • Send a frame, get a risk readout.
  • Perfect for driveways, yards, and private roads.
