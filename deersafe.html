What DeerSafe Does

DeerSafe is a visual intelligence instrument that detects wildlife, anomalies, and collision risks in real time. It is engineered for drivers, fleets, insurers, and operators who need reliable, high-speed detection.

  • Real-time wildlife detection
  • Collision-risk scoring
  • Anomaly classification
  • Heatmap and event exports
  • Telemetry overlays
  • Operator-grade performance

Drivers

Reduce incidents, improve safety, and protect vehicles at scale.

Analyze wildlife risk, validate events, and reduce claims.

Researchers

Study wildlife patterns and environmental signals with precision.

How It Works

  1. Capture video or images — from dashcams or mobile devices.
  2. DeerSafe analyzes the feed — detecting wildlife, anomalies, and risk.
  3. Receive structured outputs — events, heatmaps, summaries, and telemetry.
  4. Export or integrate — for fleets, insurers, or research workflows.

Why It Matters

Wildlife collisions cost billions annually. DeerSafe provides early detection, risk intelligence, and operator-grade clarity — reducing incidents and improving safety outcomes.
