Codex Labs App Building Book — The Founder’s Manual

A practical guide to building real products and operating systems on top of Codex Labs foundational infrastructure.

Coming Soon

This forthcoming manual documents how founders and product teams can build deployable applications on Codex Labs without relying on chatbot-first architectures. It focuses on concrete implementation decisions, product boundaries, and operating constraints.

The book explains why foundational infrastructure matters: derivative tools consume existing model capability, while foundational systems define how cognition is routed, interpreted, audited, and monetized across products.

Readers will get practical guidance for pricing architecture, Stripe integration patterns, reliability controls, and governance workflows needed to move from prototype to production.
